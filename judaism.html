Judaism

Exploring Jewish thought, history, and texts

I'm interested in how Jewish ideas have developed over time, how different thinkers have interpreted that tradition, how modern tools help understand Jewish texts - and, from it all, how these texts can be made relevant (or not!) to our modern lives.
