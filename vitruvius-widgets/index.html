III

Leges Aeternae

The Eternal Laws of Widget Making

01

Ordinatio

Proper arrangement of parts. Every component finds its rightful place within the whole.

02

Dispositio

The elegant distribution of elements, creating harmony through thoughtful placement.

03

Eurythmia

Beautiful appearance arising from the suitable adjustment of all members.

04

Symmetria

The correlation of each part to the whole and to each other, creating unity.

05

Decor

Appropriateness of style, ensuring each widget suits its intended purpose.

06

Distributio

Wise economy of materials and site, achieving excellence through prudent management.
